Mercenary Hiring Cost Calculator


Rules

  1. Money is to be paid up front.
  2. Combat pay is awarded on any day that has any combat, no matter how short that combat is.
  3. All mercenaries are to be paid whether they live or die. A mercenary who dies will have their pay given to their family.
  4. When hiring 10 or more mercenaries, a minimum commitment between 10-30 days is often required because it takes time to gather a force of any considerable size and mercenaries want guaranteed income if they have to travel far for assembly.
  5. When hiring 10 or more mercenaries, a wait time in days is calculated by dividined the quantity by 10, then multiplying it by 2.
  6. When hiring 10 or more mercenaries, a minimum time commitment is calculated by multiplying the wait time by 2.
  7. The promise of booty can reduce the price.
  8. If the hirer is famous, individuals or groups might offer to work for free or at a reduced cost due to the hirer's reputation.
