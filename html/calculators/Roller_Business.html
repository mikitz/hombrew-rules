Business Expenses


This is the number of days your PC spends running the business for the month.


This is the number of passionate emplyees you have. E.G. family members, founders, co-founders, profit-share employees, etc.


This is the number employees you have that share in the profits of your business. Remember that each one should be added to the 'Number of Employees' input box as well.

Rules

  1. This follows the rules in the DMG on pg. 129 with the following modifications.
  2. The number of passionate employees is added to the d100.
  3. Passionate employees are generally employees with profit-share agreements, so the total profit is split amongst them.
