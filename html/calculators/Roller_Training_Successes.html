Training Successes Needed

This Roller computes the number off successful skill checks you need to reach the selected skill level. When using this roller, you can take excess successes back as unused training hours. E.G. if you need 12 more successes in order to obtain proficiency in Medicine but wind up with Total Success = 19, you can take 19 - 12 = 7 hours back into you unused training hours.

Inputs



Use the ability score that is associated with the skill you wish to train.


Training Roller

This calculator computes the total successes you roll from a number of hours you spend training a single skill.

Inputs



Use the ability score that is associated with the skill you wish to train.




Rules for Training a Skill

When the player uses the website to roll for their training, they can take excess successes back as unused training hours. E.G. if a PC needs 12 more successes in order to obtain proficiency in Medicine but uses the site's roller and winds up with Total Success = 19, they can take 19 - 12 = 7 hours back into their unused training hours.

Rolling

  1. Make a Skill Check with the skill you wish to train.
  2. A natural 20 means you gain 2 successes.
  3. A natural 1 means you lose 1 success.

Determining Number of Successes Needed

Proficiency

  1. Number of Required Successes = 100 - ((Intelligence Modifier + Ability Modifier) * 10)

Expertise

  1. Number of Required Successes = 200 - ((Intelligence Modifier + Ability Modifier) * 10)

Difficult Class

Proficiency

DC = 15

Expertise

DC = 20
