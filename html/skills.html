Skill Training

When the player uses the website to roll for their training, they can take excess successes back as unused training hours. E.G. if a PC needs 12 more successes in order to obtain proficiency in Medicine but uses the site's roller and winds up with Total Success = 19, they can take 19 - 12 = 7 hours back into their unused training hours.
  • Nat 20 on the check means you gain 2 hrs
  • Nat 1 means you lose an hour
  • Proficiency
    1. starts at 100 hrs (minimum 10 hours)
    2. from the 100 hrs subtract your Int. mod. times 10 and the Ability mod. that is used in the skill your training times 10.
      •  Example
        • I want to train Survival, which uses Wisdom.
        • I have a Wisdom mod. of +2 and an Int. mod of +0.
        • So, it’ll take me 80 hours of study.
    3. DC 15
  • Expertise
    1. Starts at 200 hrs (minimum 20 hours)
    2. from the 200 hrs subtract your Int. mod. times 10 and the Ability mod. that is used in the skill your training times 10.
      •  Example
        • I want to train Survival, which uses Wisdom.
        • I have a Wisdom mod. of +2 and an Int. mod of +1.
        • So, it’ll take me 170 hours of study.
    3. DC 20

Animal Friendship

The character must use the skill in the Skill column with their target creature, spending 1 hour to do so and succeed on that check as many times as is listed in the Successes column.
A success is defined as any check total that is equal to or greater than the number listed in the DC column.
