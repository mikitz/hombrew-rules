Crafting

Gold per Day

  • The amount of gold one can produce in a day is equal to your proficiency bonus (assuming you have proficiency in the relevant tool) plus your Intelligence modifier, then times that sum by 10.
    • E.G. I have a proficiency bonus of +7 and an Int. mod. of +5. So, I add 7 and 5 to get 12, then multiply it by 10, equalling 120gp of crafting per 8 hours of work per day.

Crafting Constructs

  • Requirements
    • Control Gem with a CR limit equal to or greater than the Creature
    • Materials equal to Creature CR * CR + 1, then times 100, finally divide by 2 ((CR*(CR+1)*100)/2
  • Repairing
    • Depends on size
      • Tiny: 15 seconds per HP
      • Small: 30 seconds per HP
      • Medium: 1 minute per HP
      • Large: 2 minutes per HP
      • Huge: 4 minutes per HP
      • Gargantuan: 8 minutes per HP
  • Can craft creatures with a CR equal to the Artificer’s level

Turning a Spell into a Magic Item

Instructions

  • 1 spell scroll per charge
  • 1 item of your choosing
  • A spellcaster with access to said spell
    • 1 spell slot equal to the number of charges must be used to enchant the item per charge
      1. if the number of charges is more than 9, then spell slots can be combined with their sum equalling the total number of charge

Crafting Duration

  • 8 hours per spell slot level per charge

Calculating Cost

  • Spell scrolls (See Spell Scrolls table above.)
  • Spell slots (See Cost (For Service))
